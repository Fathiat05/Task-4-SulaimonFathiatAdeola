# Task-4-SulaimonFathiatAdeola
**Retail Business Performance Dashboard**
This project is a power bi dashboard designed to analyze retail business performance and provide insights into sales, revenue,customer behaviour and operational efficiency.
**Tools Used**
Power BI 
DAX
Data Modeling
Data Visualisation
**Key Metrics**
Total Revenue
Average Items per Cart
Average Unit Price
Total Units Sold
**Key Insights**
Identify top performance products based on sales volume and revenue.
Analyzed customer payment preference.
Evaluated order fulfillment status across different categories.
Assessed the effectiveness of referral channels in driving sales.
**Project Outcome**
The dashboard provides a clear overview of retail business performance,enabling stakeholders to monitor key metrics,identify trends and make data driven decision.
